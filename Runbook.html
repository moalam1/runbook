Go/No-Go Criteria
ℹ️
This runbook governs the end-to-end migration from Adobe Managed Services (AMS) to AEM as a Cloud Service (AEMaaCS). The Go/No-Go meeting is on 10th June.

Business Drivers

  • Gain auto-scaling, zero-downtime deployments, and built-in global CDN (Akamai)
  • Enable continuous delivery via Cloud Manager pipelines
  • Comply with Adobe's latest SDK standards and security requirements
  • Reduce operational burden on the platform engineering team

Rollback Plan

⚠️
Keep AMS in maintenance mode for 72 hours post-cutover. This is your rollback safety net. Rollback authority: Nishant or Dev Lead.

Rollback Triggers

  • Production error rate sustained for more than 10 minutes
  • Response time on any core user journey
  • Critical functionality unavailable
  • Data integrity issues — content missing or corrupted post-cutover
  • Security incident detected on new environment
  • Site unavailable for continuous minutes after go-live
  • Akamai CDN not serving correct content after DNS propagation
